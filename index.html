Aiswarya Dutta



A person I can't afford to lose


Things I love about you


Smile

Smile so pretty that even sadness fears to go near.

Eyes

How lucky am I, to be the person who looks into your eyes?

...actually you

The list wont end this way, so yes I love you as a whole and I find you beautiful inside out.

Everytime I get a chance to choose,I'll always choose you.






I dedicate you this song.


I love you Aiswarya, more than you can ever imagine and you will always be my one. Thank you for being there beside me and I wish you a very Happy Birthday with all the love possible and the hope that I will do the same for many years to come.
